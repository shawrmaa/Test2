About Us

Overview

This website is created to raise awareness about endangered wildlife in the Philippines. It highlights different species and encourages people to help protect our environment.

About Us

We are students from PLMun who developed this project as part of our academic requirement. Our goal is to educate others and promote wildlife conservation through simple and accessible information.

Our Mission

Our mission is to spread awareness, inspire action, and encourage people to take part in protecting endangered species and preserving biodiversity.
