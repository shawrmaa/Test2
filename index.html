Philippine Wildlife
Conservation

OVERVIEW

The Philippines is one of the most biodiverse countries in the world, home to many unique species found nowhere else. These animals play a vital role in maintaining the balance of ecosystems and supporting life.

However, many species are now endangered due to deforestation, illegal hunting, pollution, and climate change. These threats continue to put wildlife at serious risk.

Protecting wildlife helps preserve biodiversity, maintain ecological balance, and secure a better future for the next generation.
